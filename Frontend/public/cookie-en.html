Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy supplements the Privacy Policy and describes how this site uses cookies and similar technologies in accordance with the GDPR and ePrivacy legislation.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites may store on your device. They are used for site operation, to remember preferences, or for analytics and marketing, where your consent is required.

2. Types of cookies and similar technologies used

This site uses only essential and functional technologies. There is no cookie consent banner and no third-party analytics or marketing services.

TypePurposeConsent
Local storage (localStorage)To remember your language choice (Italian, English, Spanish) for your next visit. Cookie-like technology, stored only on your device.Not required (functional preference).
Technical cookiesAny cookies strictly necessary for site operation and security (e.g. load balancing).Not required (legitimate interest).

3. Third-party cookies

This site does not currently use third-party cookies for analytics or marketing. If third-party services (e.g. Google Analytics or fonts from external CDNs) were added in the future, related cookies would only be used with your explicit consent. For any external services, see their respective policies (e.g. Google Privacy).

4. Duration and management

The language preference (localStorage) persists until you change it or clear the site’s data in your browser. You can clear cookies and local data at any time from your browser settings (e.g. History → Clear browsing data).

5. Your rights

In relation to data processed via cookies you may exercise the rights under the GDPR (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, complaint). For more information see the Privacy Policy.
